Nowadays we can see the phrase 'breast cancer' popping out everywhere and it's no surprise with it being the 2nd highest 
type of cancer after lung cancer amongst the women. So the head of the well renowned private hospital has put a special
emphasis on predicting the stage or the severity of cancer which will help in providing personalized care and remedies to
all the patients thereby accordingly focussing the attention towards serious cases and thereby better handling of the 
ailment. So, based on this business idea the approach is selected and it is planned to create a machine learning model to 
predict the severity The target audience will obviously be the doctors and subsequently the patients and it will assist 
the already experienced doctors to better handle the cases.
