The transport agency has outlined the increase in the number of road accident cases nowadays. It has planned on devising
a way wherein it has aimed to provide particular kind of treatment and solutions for different scenarios. Its aim is to
provide special attention which can be equipping the area with ambulance and other medical facility or diverting the 
traffic where there higher severity of crashes. The task will be to predict the severity of these car accidents so as to
be ready with optimum safety arrangements or planning the traffic in that area or during that time. The study will 
determine the key factors leading to decide the severity of the car accident.
